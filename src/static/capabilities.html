Core Capabilities

Engineered to solve the specific challenges medical professionals face with traditional AI systems.

Multimodal Understanding

Placebo AI understands complex medical layouts—not just text. It converts dosage tables, clinical trial data, pharmacokinetic parameters, chemical structures, and metabolic pathways into structured, queryable intelligence.

Semantic Search

Finds meaning, not just keywords. It understands the intent behind questions—matching "glucose-lowering drugs" to the correct antihyperglycemic agents even when keywords don't overlap.

Citation-Backed Answers

Every claim includes a direct link to its source textbook page. No unsubstantiated statements. Professional medical researchers can open the original source to verify data instantly.

High-Speed Intelligence

Delivers medical intelligence in seconds. Optimized for interactive use during clinical rounds or while reviewing patient cases, providing answers significantly faster than manual textbook lookups.
