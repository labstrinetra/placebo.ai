The Future of Medical Intelligence

Placebo AI is a next-generation medical intelligence system built to bridge the gap between complex pharmaceutical data and real-world clinical decision-making.

Vision
Bridging Data & Decision
Turning complex medical textbooks into structured, searchable, and 100% auditable knowledge bases. We believe in a transparent "glass box" approach to medical intelligence.
Safety
Zero Hallucination
Strict "Retrieved Context Only" rule to ensure 100% factual accuracy in clinical answers.
Intelligence
Molecular Precision
Deep semantic understanding of pharmaceutical structures, metabolic pathways, and drug-drug interactions extracted directly from peer-reviewed literature.
Anatomy
Visual Grounding
Multimodal extraction of anatomical charts and diagrams to provide visual context for every medical response.
